White Nebula is an international team of high school students focused on learning computer science and cybersecurity. Driven by motivation and ambitious goals, the team develops a wide range of technical skills and applies them in real-world competitions. It’s a reciprocal relationship where all members help each other improve, fostering both individual growth and collective success through teamwork and problem-solving.

"It all began during our third year of high school. I remember how the two of us, filled with nothing but raw curiosity, decided to give cybersecurity and CTFs a try. We found an event, registered, and spent the entire weekend solving challenges—little sleep and hardly any knowledge, but we persevered. I can still feel the rush from cracking that zip file after brute forcing through 17 billion possible combinations.

From that spark, everything just expanded. We jumped into more events—competitive coding, national competitions, even worldwide challenges—and even dabbled in hacking. But White Nebula grew into something more than just a team; it became a bond that carried over into every project we touched.

Now, after all these adventures, we’re still inspired to learn. The thrill of overcoming those daunting challenges is unlike anything else. As we await our university decisions, our paths may soon diverge, but I believe we have an important message for the youth: dare to try, be creative, and follow your passion—just like we did on that unforgettable Friday night." - Klart
So what is, really, White Nebula?
White Nebula is not just a team, It is a boundless constellation of unity, where stars align to ignite the universe of collaboration.
